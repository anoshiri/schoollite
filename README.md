## About School Lite

School Lite is a simple grade school management system targeted at Nigerian schools. It's features will include the following:

- ** Students record**
- ** Teachers**
- ** Grades and arms**
- ** Subjects**
- ** Exams and scores**
- ** Parents/guardians**
- ** Automatic attendance**
- ** Teachers/Parents Correspondence**
- ** More**
